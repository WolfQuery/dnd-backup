# Backstory
---
The doctor was once a respected physician and scholar in their homeland, dedicated to the study of life and death. They believed that the soul could be purified through the body, and that death was simply an imbalance, a disease that could be cured. This obsession led them down a dark path, experimenting with forbidden practices in search of the elusive cure for mortality.
